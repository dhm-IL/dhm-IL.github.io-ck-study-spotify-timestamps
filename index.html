How to use

  1. Connect Spotify.
  2. Open the Spotify app on the participant device, then return to this page.
  3. Select the participant and playlist.
  4. Enter the starting temperature and humidity.
  5. Click Start Study Session.
  6. At the end, click Finish Session, enter the ending temperature and humidity, then download and share the data file.
